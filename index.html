The Problem

Aging HDB flats mean frequent repairs, loud drilling, and even forced relocations. Each year, over 100,000 residents face disruptions, costing Singapore nearly S$600M annually. Elderly residents and families are most affected by stress, noise, and safety risks.

Our Solution

Step 1: Drill

A portable device is placed against the wall to create a small entry point.

Step 2: Insert & Release

The device injects self-healing chemicals and nano-coatings into the structure, sealing cracks and insulating noise.

Step 3: Heal

Walls recover strength, extend lifespan by 30–60+ years, and minimize disruption to residents.
